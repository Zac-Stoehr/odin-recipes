Tikka Masala Recipe

Description

This is a super simple tikka masala recipe that works with any protein!

Ingredients

  • 1 can Cocunut Milk or cream
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 1Tbs tomato paste
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1Tbs graham masala
  • 1 medium onion

Steps

  1. dice and saute onion until soft
  2. add tomato paste and spices and cook for 45sec
  3. add cans of tomato, cocunut milk, and chickpeas
  4. simmer for 30mins

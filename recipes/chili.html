Chili Recipe

Description

This is an easy to make and flexible recipe to help you make great chili on a budget

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 1lb protein of your chice
  • 1Tbs Tomato paste
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2cans Chili beans
  • Chili powder to taste

Steps

  1. Dice and saute onion on medium heat until transluscent
  2. Add protein and cook
  3. Add everything else and bring to a simmer
  4. Cook for at least 30min

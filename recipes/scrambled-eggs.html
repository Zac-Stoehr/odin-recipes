Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

A simple technique based scramble egg recipe that will have your guests asking, "How did yiu make thes eggs so creamy!?"

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Greek Yoghurt

Steps

  1. Preheat pan on medium-high heat
  2. add 1tsp butter
  3. add eggs to pan
  4. stir continuosly untill eggs are almost cooked
  5. add 1tsp greek yoghurt to slow cooking process
